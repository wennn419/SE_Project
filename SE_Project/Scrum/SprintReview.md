# Sprint Review

## Introduction

Sprint Review meetings were conducted at the end of each sprint to evaluate the completed work, demonstrate the implemented features, and determine whether the sprint objectives had been successfully achieved. During each review session, the team presented the completed product increment, discussed development outcomes, gathered feedback, and identified improvements for the following sprint.

Unlike Sprint Planning, which focused on defining development objectives, the Sprint Review focused on assessing the actual functionality delivered during the sprint. Each completed feature was evaluated against the planned sprint goal to ensure that the project continued to meet user requirements and overall project objectives.

Through three Sprint Review sessions, the team successfully monitored project progress, validated completed features, and continuously improved the Fitness Workout Tracker application following Scrum Agile practices.

---

# Sprint 1 Review

## Sprint Goal

The goal of Sprint 1 was to establish the foundation of the Fitness Workout Tracker application by implementing the project's core features, including the Basic BMI Assessment System, Calendar Feature, Achievement Badge System, and the initial user interface.

---

## Sprint Goal Achievement

The Sprint 1 objective was successfully achieved. All planned core modules were implemented and integrated into the application, providing users with the essential functionality required for the initial version of the Fitness Workout Tracker.

The successful completion of these features established a stable development foundation for future enhancement and integration activities.

---

## Completed Features

The following features were successfully completed during Sprint 1.

### BMI Assessment System

- Users can enter their height and weight.
- The system calculates the user's Body Mass Index (BMI).
- BMI category is displayed immediately after calculation.

### Calendar Feature

- Monthly workout calendar implemented.
- Users can schedule workouts.
- Workout information can be viewed directly from the calendar.

### Achievement Badge System

- Basic achievement badges were implemented.
- Users can unlock badges after completing workout activities.
- Badge information is displayed within the application.

### User Interface

- Initial UI/UX layout completed.
- Navigation between project modules implemented.
- Consistent interface design established for future development.

---

## Feature Demonstration

During the Sprint Review meeting, the completed features were demonstrated to verify that the Sprint 1 objectives had been achieved.

The demonstration confirmed that:

- The BMI Assessment System calculated BMI values correctly.
- The Calendar Feature successfully displayed workout schedules.
- Achievement badges could be unlocked after completing workout activities.
- Users were able to navigate between project modules through the application interface.

Overall, the demonstration verified that all Sprint 1 deliverables functioned as expected and provided a stable foundation for subsequent development.

---

## Sprint Outcome

Sprint 1 successfully delivered the minimum functional version of the Fitness Workout Tracker application. The implementation of the core modules enabled users to perform essential fitness-related activities while providing the development team with a stable platform for future feature enhancements.

All planned Sprint 1 objectives were completed within the intended development period.

---

## What Went Well

Several positive outcomes were identified during the Sprint 1 Review:

- Team members completed their assigned responsibilities according to the sprint plan.
- Collaboration between developers enabled successful integration of the initial project modules.
- The application's core functionality operated correctly during feature demonstrations.
- The initial user interface provided a clear and consistent user experience.
- Communication throughout Sprint 1 supported efficient task coordination and progress monitoring.

---

## Challenges Encountered

Although Sprint 1 was completed successfully, several minor challenges were identified during the review.

- Minor interface inconsistencies were observed after integrating individual project modules.
- Additional improvements to the user interface were identified to enhance usability.
- Some project modules required further refinement before implementing advanced functionality.

These challenges did not prevent the successful completion of Sprint 1 and were scheduled for improvement during Sprint 2.

---

## Improvements for Sprint 2

Based on the Sprint Review discussion, the team agreed to focus on the following improvements during Sprint 2:

- Enhance the BMI Recommendation System.
- Improve the Calendar Feature with additional functionality.

---

## Conclusion

Sprint 1 successfully established the core functionality of the Fitness Workout Tracker application. The completed modules fulfilled the planned sprint objectives and provided a strong development foundation for subsequent enhancement activities.

The Sprint Review confirmed that the project was progressing according to schedule and that the team was ready to proceed with Sprint 2.
